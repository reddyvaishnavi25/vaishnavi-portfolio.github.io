Advancements in Taste Prediction using Facial Expression Analysis

Tech Stack: Python, Tensorflow, OpenCV, DCNN, ResNet , VGG16, MobileNet

Github URL: Project Link

  • Utilize the Fer2013 dataset to analyze facial expressions and correlate them with taste perceptions.
  • Implement deep convolutional neural networks (DCNN) such as ResNet, VGG16, and MobileNet for taste estimation.
  • Map facial emotions from the dataset to taste categories including sweet, savory, bitter, and hot.
  • Train and evaluate the DCNN models to determine their effectiveness in accurately estimating taste based on facial expressions.
